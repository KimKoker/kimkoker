Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Kim Koker Analytics ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal analytics tool used solely by Kim Koker to track social media performance metrics. This application does not collect, store, or share data from third parties.

2. Information We Collect

This application accesses only the account owner's own social media analytics data, including:

  • Follower counts
  • Video performance metrics (views, likes, comments, shares)
  • Engagement rates

3. How We Use Information

All data accessed through this application is used exclusively for personal analytics and tracking purposes by the account owner. No data is shared with third parties, sold, or used for any commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only by the account owner. Google's privacy policy and security measures apply to this stored data.

5. Third-Party Services

This application uses APIs from:

  • TikTok
  • Instagram (Meta)

These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data collection and use.

6. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect access to your data. API credentials are stored securely in Google Apps Script and are not exposed publicly.

7. Your Rights

As the sole user of this application, you have complete control over your data. You may delete any collected data at any time by deleting the associated Google Sheet or revoking API access.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the application administrator.
